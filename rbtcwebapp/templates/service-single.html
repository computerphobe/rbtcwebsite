    EVENTS

    Stay updated on all the latest activities of the Robotics & AI Club! Dive into engaging workshops, hands-on sessions, and exciting events that foster innovation and learning in the field of robotics and AI.

    TECH KRITI

    Tech Kriti Workshop, hosted by IIT Kanpur at Marwadi University, was an immersive learning experience focused on emerging technologies. Students gained hands-on exposure to advanced concepts and practical applications, guided by expert mentors from IIT Kanpur. This workshop provided a unique opportunity to enhance technical skills and explore innovative solutions in a collaborative environment.

    HACK THE MOUNTAIN

    Hack the Mountains, a high-energy hackathon hosted at Marwadi University by the Hack the Mountain team, brought together innovative minds to solve real-world challenges. Participants collaborated over intense coding sessions, developing creative solutions and showcasing their technical expertise. With mentorship, workshops, and a competitive yet inspiring environment, the hackathon provided a platform to transform ideas into impactful projects, fostering innovation and teamwork.

    WEB HORIZON

    Web Horizon, an engaging event held at Marwadi University, showcased the creativity and technical prowess of students as they designed and developed stunning website frontends. Participants demonstrated their skills in web design, blending innovation with functionality to create user-centric interfaces. The event fostered a collaborative and competitive atmosphere, encouraging students to push the boundaries of their web development expertise.

    API CHALLENGE

    API X Postman Challenge, hosted at Marwadi University, was an exciting event where participants built dynamic websites using APIs and explored their functionalities through Postman. Students showcased their skills in integrating APIs seamlessly into web applications, emphasizing innovation and practical implementation. The event provided a platform to enhance API knowledge, promote creativity, and foster collaboration among tech enthusiasts.

    INTELLIFY

    INTELLIFY Hackathon, held at Marwadi University, was a transformative tech event where participants tackled real-world challenges with innovative solutions. Over an intense coding marathon, students collaborated to develop cutting-edge projects, blending creativity with technology. The hackathon fostered a spirit of innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving, providing a platform for future tech leaders to shine.

    GO DRONE

    Go Drone, an exciting drone racing event held at Marwadi University, featured participants who built their own drones and raced them through a challenging obstacle course. Competitors demonstrated their engineering and piloting skills as they navigated their custom-built drones through obstacles, testing speed, precision, and innovation. The event offered a thrilling platform for participants to showcase their creativity and technical expertise in drone design and racing.

    ROBO COMP

    RoboComp, the flagship event of the Robotics & AI Club at Marwadi University, is a thrilling robotics competition that brings together innovation, teamwork, and technology. Participants design and program their own robots to tackle exciting challenges, showcasing their creativity and technical expertise. With diverse rounds testing speed, precision, and problem-solving, RoboComp provides a platform for students to explore cutting-edge robotics while fostering collaboration and competitive spirit. Join us to experience the future of robotics in action!
