    NRF With Antenna

    The NRF series is a set of low-power RF modules for wireless communication. The module typically comes with an antenna for longer-range communication.

    RF Transceiver

    A low-power device for transmitting and receiving radio frequency signals, enabling bidirectional communication with an integrated antenna for extended range.

    GPS module

    A module that receives GPS signals to provide geolocation data, including latitude, longitude, and time. Used in navigation, tracking, and mapping applications.

    Bluetooth Module

    A module that enables Bluetooth wireless communication between devices. It is commonly used for connecting devices like smartphones to microcontrollers.

    NodeMCU Shield

    A biometric sensor used for fingerprint recognition. It is used for access control and security systems. Fingerprint authentication, security systems, attendance tracking.

    WiFi Module

    A wireless module that enables devices to connect to a Wi-Fi network, often used in IoT projects to transmit data wirelessly. IoT, wireless communication.

    Raspberry Pi (Nano)

    A small, affordable computer that can run Linux and interact with sensors and actuators in various DIY projects. IoT, media centers, home automation, robotics.

    Arduino Nano

    Arduino Nano A small, breadboard-compatible microcontroller board based on the ATmega328. Ideal for compact electronics projects, DIY sensors, and robotics.

    Accelerometer

    A sensor that measures the acceleration forces acting on a body, which could be used to detect motion, tilt, or orientation. Motion detection, mobile phones.

    Ultrasonic Sensor

    A sensor that measures distance by emitting ultrasonic waves and measuring the time it takes for them to bounce back. It's commonly used for object detection.

    Gas Sensor

    A sensor used to detect the presence and concentration of various gases such as methane, carbon monoxide, or LPG. safety systems, home automation.

    PIR Sensor

    PIR Sensor A sensor that detects infrared light from moving objects, commonly used for motion detection in security systems, automation, and lighting controls.

    DHT Sensor

    A digital temperature and humidity sensor. It provides temperature and humidity readings for various applications. Weather stations, indoor air quality monitoring.

    IR Sensor

    IR Sensor A light-sensitive device that changes resistance based on light intensity. Used for light sensing, object detection, and proximity sensing in various applications.

    Touch Sensor

    A sensor that detects physical touch or proximity. It is often used as a user input interface for electronics. Touch-based control systems, user interfaces.

    Soil Moisture Sensor

    A sensor that detects the moisture content in the soil, commonly used for irrigation systems or agriculture monitoring. Gardening, agriculture, smart irrigation.

    Range Sensor

    A sensor used to detect the pulse rate or heartbeats by measuring the electrical signals from the heart. Health monitoring, fitness trackers, medical applications.

    Pulse Sensor

    A sensor used to detect the pulse rate or heartbeats by measuring the electrical signals from the heart. Health monitoring, fitness trackers, medical applications.

    Finger Sensor

    A biometric sensor used for fingerprint recognition. It is used for access control and security systems. Fingerprint authentication, security systems, attendance tracking.

    Flag Sensor

    Flag Sensor Flag Sensor A sensor used to detect flags or moving objects, commonly used in automation systems, robotic tracking, and object detection applications for monitoring and control.

    Microphone Sensor

    A sensor that picks up sound waves and converts them into electrical signals, often used for audio input in electronics. Voice recognition, sound detection.

    Turbidity Sensor

    A sensor that measures the turbidity (clarity) of liquids by detecting suspended particles. Often used in water quality monitoring systems. Water quality testing.

    PH Module

    A sensor used to measure the pH level of a liquid, indicating its acidity or alkalinity. Essential for water quality monitoring, agriculture, and industrial processes.

    ECG Sensor

    An Electrocardiogram sensor that measures the electrical activity of the heart. Used for health monitoring and diagnostics. Health monitoring, wearable devices.

    LDR Sensor

    A type of variable resistor that changes resistance based on the intensity of light falling on it. It is used for light sensing. Light-sensitive devices.

    Relay (4 Channel)

    A relay module with four channels, allowing multiple devices to be controlled by a low-voltage signal. Used to switch high-voltage devices.

    Servo Motor (Mini)

    A small servomotor used for precise position control, often in robotics and hobby projects. Robotics, control systems, DIY projects.

    9V battery

    The nine-volt battery, or 9-volt battery, is an electric battery that supplies a nominal voltage of 9 volts. Actual voltage measures 7.2 to 9.6 volts.

    Cell

    1.5 V batteries for general use with lamps, radios, etc. They must be alkaline. Saline types are now abandoned. Don’t charge non-rechargeable types.

    Battery Cap(9V)

    The main battery terminals are the positive and negative posts. The battery may also have vent caps on top of it, Used over only a 9V battery

    Lithium ion cell

    A Lithium-ion cell is defined as a rechargeable cell that utilizes lithium ions moving between electrodes during charging and discharging processes.

    Servo Motor (Mega)

    Description: A larger servomotor capable of handling more torque and power than a mini servo, used for larger robotic or mechanical applications.

    Servo Driver

    A controller used to operate multiple servo motors simultaneously, often used in large robotic systems. Robotics, automation, control systems.

    Motor Driver (L293N)

    A motor driver IC that allows you to control DC motors, stepper motors, and other types of motors using low-power control signals from microcontrollers.

    N20 Motor

    A small DC motor used in various mechanical tasks, like robotics, automation, and precision applications. Ideal for robotics, DIY machines, and actuating components.

    DC Fans

    A fan powered by direct current, used for cooling purposes. It can be controlled for speed and direction of airflow. Cooling systemsheat dissipation.

    Servo Driver (8 Channel)

    A driver that controls multiple servo motors , allowing precise movement and control of robotic joints or actuators. Robotics, automation, control.

    Battery Adapter

    A component used to interface with batteries to supply power to electronic circuits. ∙ Power supply for microcontrollers, mobile projects.

    Transceiver

    A controller used to operate multiple servo motors simultaneously, often used in large robotic systems. Robotics, automation, control systems.

    USB Module

    A module designed to allow devices with microcomputers to communicate via USB. It enables USB devices to be connected to a microcontroller.

    Traffic Lights Module

    A traffic light module with LEDs to simulate the lights of a traffic signal (red, yellow, green). It's often used in projects involving traffic control.

    Joystick

    A device that detects movement in multiple directions and sends that data to a microcontroller. It's often used in gaming or robotic control.

    Knock Sensor Module

    A sensor used to detect vibrations or knocks. It is often used in security systems or interactive devices. Security systems, interactive electronics.

    LCD (16x2)

    A 16x2 liquid crystal display (LCD) screen used for displaying text. It can show up to 32 characters and is commonly used in microcontroller projects.

    I2C (Inter-Integrated Circuit)

    OLED Display

    Laser Module

    A laser diode module used to emit a focused laser beam. It can be used for distance measurement, laser engraving, positioning, alignment, or other applications.

    Potentiometer

    A variable resistor, often used to adjust the level of electrical resistance, which can control voltage or current in a circuit. It's often used as an input device.

    Arduino Nano Cable

    A cable used to connect the Arduino Nano board to a computer or power supply. Often used for uploading code or powering the Arduino.

    USB - B Cable

    A USB cable with a USB-B connector on one end, often used to connect devices like printers or external hard drives to a computer.

    A cable used to connect devices with a micro HDMI output to an HDMI compatible device . Connecting Raspberry Pi or Jetson Nano to monitors.
